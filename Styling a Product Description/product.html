Laptop

Laptops are designed to be portable computers. They are smaller and lighter than desktops. The name connotes the user's ability to put the computer in their lap while they use it. Laptops have rechargeable batteries that can be used for a set period away from a power source.

Testing conducted by Apple in May 2022 using pre-production 13‑inch MacBook Pro systems with Apple M2, 8‑core CPU, 10‑core GPU, 8GB of RAM and 256GB SSD. The wireless web test measures battery life by wirelessly browsing 25 popular websites with display brightness set to 8 clicks from bottom.
